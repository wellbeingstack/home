About Xiao Yue

I've been where you are.

I spent two decades navigating tech's relentless pace — through hypergrowth, layoffs, leadership pressure, and the constant hum of the AI race. I worked at companies like Meta, managed large teams, and for a long time, let work consume far more than it should have.

Eventually I found a better way. Not a perfect way — but a more intentional, sustainable one. Now I want to help others find theirs, without having to spend years figuring it out alone.

I started WellbeingStack because no one should have to sacrifice their health, relationships, or joy to have a meaningful career in tech.
